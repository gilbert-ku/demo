MY EXPERIENCE LIVING IN KIBERA.

I was born in Kibera some times back and this is where I started schooling before we relocate to upcountry. I came back in 2018 after I finished my form four studies. I came with a purpose of looking of employment and unfortunaly, the rate of unemployment in slam is so high. The easiest employment you can get here is working for a constration site, making less than $3 per day. Due to that, most resident in Kibera slam live in extreme poverty.
